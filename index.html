OUR STORY

Built on a Mission
to Empower Africa

3WISE Research Hub was born from a simple but powerful belief: that Africa's greatest decisions deserve Africa's best research. We saw brilliant students struggling with data, businesses making million-naira choices on guesswork, and NGOs writing proposals without evidence. We decided to change that.

Founded by Samuel Stephen, our firm brings global research standards to local contexts — combining rigorous academic methodology with deep, boots-on-the-ground knowledge of African markets, cultures, and challenges.

Today, we serve clients across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, and 12+ African countries, delivering research that doesn't just sit in reports — it drives real decisions, secures real funding, and creates real impact.
